Dino

A game based on the intergrated game in the Chrome web brower. When the user is offline, a T-rex dinosaur appears on the screen. When press the space bar, the dinosaur will jump. To play, you need to control the dinosaur to avoid the cactuses.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe or noughts and crosses in other contries is a paper-and pencil game. Two players (X and O) take turns to fill in the grid. The player who has three of their marks in a diagonal, horizontal or vertical row wins.
